## Условие
$$
\begin{cases}
x'' = 2y \\
y'' = -2x
\end{cases}
$$
## Решение
$$
\begin{cases}
x''  + 0x - 2y = 0 \\
2x + y'' + 0y = 0
\end{cases}
$$
$$
\begin{vmatrix}
\lambda^{2} & -2 \\
2 &  \lambda^{2}
\end{vmatrix} = \lambda^{4} +4 = 0
$$
$$
\begin{align}
\lambda_{1, 2} = 1 \pm i \\
\lambda_{3, 4} = -1 \pm i
\end{align}
$$

### $\lambda_{1, 2} = 1 + i$
$$
\begin{cases}
2i\alpha - 2\beta = 0 & \alpha = -i\beta \\
2\alpha + 2i\beta = 0 & \beta = \beta = i
\end{cases} \quad V_{1} = \begin{pmatrix}
1 \\
i
\end{pmatrix} 
$$
$$
e^{1 + i}\begin{pmatrix}
1 \\
i
\end{pmatrix}
$$

### $\lambda_{3, 4} = -1 + i$
$$
\begin{cases}
-2i\alpha - 2\beta = 0 & \alpha = i\beta \\
2\alpha - 2i\beta = 0 & \beta = \beta = i
\end{cases} \quad V_{2} = \begin{pmatrix}
-1 \\
i
\end{pmatrix} 
$$
