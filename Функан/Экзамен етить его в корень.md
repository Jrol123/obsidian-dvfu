$$
(X, \rho) - \text{метрическое пространство}
$$
$$
K \subset X : \forall \{ x_{k} \}_{k=1}^{{\infty}} \subset K \quad \exists\{ x_{k_{j}} \}_{j=1}^{\infty} : x_{k_{j}} \to x\in K
