# Брюсселятор
**Первый закон Фика**
$$
I= - D \cdot \nabla C
$$

$$
m = C \cdot V = C \cdot S \cdot \Delta r
$$
$$
\dfrac{\Delta m}{\Delta t} = (I_{in} - I_{out}) \cdot S
$$
$$
\dfrac{\Delta m}{\Delta t} = \dfrac{\Delta C}{\Delta t} \cdot S \cdot \Delta r
$$
$$
I_{in} - I_{out} = \dfrac{\Delta C}{\Delta t} \cdot \Delta r
$$
$$
\dfrac{\partial C}{\partial t} = \dfrac{I_{in} - I_{out}}{\Delta r} = - \dfrac{\partial I}{\partial r}
$$
юзаем закон фика
$$
I = -D\Delta C = -D \dfrac{\partial C}{\partial r}
$$
$$
\dfrac{\partial C}{\partial t} = D \dfrac{\partial^2 C}{\partial r^2}
$$
**Уравнение реакции-дифузии**
$$
\dot x = f(x) + D \cdot x_{rr}
$$
**В общем виде**
$$
\dot x_i = f(x_1, \ldots, x_n) + D \cdot \dfrac{\partial^2 x_i}{\partial r^2}
$$

$$
\cases{
\dot x = P(x, y) + x_{rr} \\
\dot y = Q(x, y) + y_{rr}
}
$$
**Брюсселятор**
$$
A, B \underset{X, Y}{\to} D, E
$$
$$
A \to Y \quad k_1
$$
$$
2X + Y \to 3X \quad k_2
$$
$$
B + X \to Y + D \quad k_3
$$
$$
X \to E \quad k_4
$$
$$
\cases{
\dot X = k_1 A + k_2 X^2 Y - k_3 B X - k_4 X \\
\dot Y = -k_2 X^2 Y + k_3 B X 
}
$$
