# Брюсселятор
**Первый закон Фика**
$$
I= - D \cdot \nabla C
$$

$$
m = C \cdot V = C \cdot S \cdot \Delta r
$$
$$
\dfrac{\Delta m}{\Delta t} = (I_{in} - I_{out}) \cdot S
$$
$$
\dfrac{\Delta m}{\Delta t} = \dfrac{\Delta C}{\Delta t} \cdot S \cdot \Delta r
$$
$$
I_{in} - I_{out} = \dfrac{\Delta C}{\Delta t} \cdot \Delta r
$$
$$
\dfrac{\partial C}{\partial t} = \dfrac{I_{in} - I_{out}}{\Delta r}
$$
