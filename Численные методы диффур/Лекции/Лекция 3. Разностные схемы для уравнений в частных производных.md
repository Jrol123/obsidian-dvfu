\dfrac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial t^{2}} = a^{2}\dfrac{\partial^{2}u}{\partial x^{2}}
$$
# Эллиптические
$$
\dfrac{\partial u}{\partial t}=a\dfrac{\partial^2 u}{\partial x^2} \quad a>0
$$
# Параболические
$$
